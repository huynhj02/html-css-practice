Images

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPGs

JPGs are good for photos.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations.

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams/icons, since they share the same colour abilities as JPGs and can be transparent. However, for a PNG to appear the same quality as a JPG, it will take up a much larger file size.

SVGs

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can.
